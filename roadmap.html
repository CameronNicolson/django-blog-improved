Roadmap¶

The roadmap outlines the crucial tasks needed to achieve each milestone in the release cycle, serving as a guide to success.

The overarching goal is to provide a flexible blog system by expanding the template with a collection of tags that enable concise blog definitions. It also aims to offer customizable styling, similar to CMS themes in WordPress, allowing any component to be tailored. Additionally, a unified interface will facilitate communication between agents, helping authors connect their content more seamlessly across platforms.
